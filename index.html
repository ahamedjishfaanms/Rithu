About Ṛtú

RITU 2025 is the second edition of the Environment Film Festival hosted by St. Joseph’s College (Autonomous), Irinjalakuda. The event is designed as a vibrant platform that celebrates, critiques, and creatively engages with pressing environmental issues through the powerful medium of cinema. Through carefully curated films, panel discussions, exhibitions, and artistic expressions, this festival aims to stir consciousness, evoke dialogue, and promote sustainable lifestyles and environmental responsibility among students, educators, and the larger community.
