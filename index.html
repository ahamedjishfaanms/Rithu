About Ṛtú

Ṛtú (meaning "season" in Sanskrit) is an annual environmental festival that brings together activists, innovators, and community members to celebrate our planet and explore sustainable solutions.

Founded in 2015, our festival has grown from a local gathering to an international event attracting over 50,000 attendees each year. We believe that through education, collaboration, and celebration, we can inspire meaningful change.

This year's theme is "Regeneration" - focusing on restoring ecosystems, rebuilding communities, and reimagining our relationship with nature.
